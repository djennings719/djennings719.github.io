This app is based on the ShootHoop project from Udemy. It ended at adding support for Oculus in a single game mode of 30 seconds to score as many baskets as possible. I added support for Google VR and multiple other game modes.

The Maze App Page

This app is the third project of the Udacity VR Nanodegree. It required you to find a key hidden in the maze before you can unlock the door at the end of the maze. You can also collect 5 coins hidden throughout as an additional challenge.
